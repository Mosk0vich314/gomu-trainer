🔥 Standard Warm-Up Routine ▼
  1. Calf Raises: Stand tall, squeeze at top.
  2. Dead Bugs, Side Planks, Bird-Dog
  3. 90/90 Stretch + Rotation: Sit with both knees at 90°, switch sides, add active breathing.
  4. Push-ups: Full plank, chest to floor, push through, pause at bottom.
  5. KB Single-Leg DLs: Hinge at hip, keep back flat, focus on balance, switch legs.
  6. KB Single-Arm Rows: Hinge forward, one arm for support, row, focus on shoulder blade.
  7. Elevated Reverse Lunges: Stepping backwards from a step, controlled descent, deep knee bend.
  8. Band Dislocates: Band overhead with wide grip, pass forward and back with straight arms.
  9. Band Y's & Pull-aparts: Target upper back and rear delts, squeeze shoulder blades.
